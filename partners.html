Pay for real results

Separate the customer offer from the KATA referral fee.

The customer receives the full discount or benefit your business chooses to offer. KATA’s referral fee is charged separately only after a verified group redemption.

Example

A group spends $200. The business offers the customers 10% off. KATA charges an $8 referral fee for bringing the group.

Group spending $200
Customer savings − $20
KATA referral fee − $8
Business revenue $172
